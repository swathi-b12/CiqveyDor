Our Story

Furniture That
Means Something

Cenova was born from a simple frustration: why does beautiful furniture have to cost a fortune? We set out to change that.

Founded 2021

Crafted in India,
Designed for the World

Cenova started in a small Bengaluru workshop with one craftsman, two designs, and a stubborn belief that premium furniture shouldn't mean premium prices. Today, we work with a network of 40 skilled artisans across Karnataka, sourcing sustainable sheesham, teak, and oak from certified forests.

Every Cenova piece goes through 27 quality checks before it reaches your home. We obsess over joinery, finish, and proportion — because great furniture isn't just seen, it's felt.
